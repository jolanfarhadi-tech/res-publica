Res Publica is an independent civic organization. Our name is our
program: *res publica* — the public matter that belongs to everyone
and concerns everyone.

## What we believe

Democracy does not live on institutions alone, but on people who take
responsibility. We create spaces where citizens, researchers, and
policymakers listen to one another — and think ahead together.

## How we work

Our work rests on three pillars: independent **research**, open
**dialogue**, and concrete **projects** of public participation. We
make all results freely available.
