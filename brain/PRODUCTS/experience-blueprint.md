# Res Publica — Experience Blueprint

**STATUS: SOURCE NOT MIGRATED — gap, not a placeholder for new content.**

The Experience Blueprint — the emotional shape of the Product Vision, produced as a 9-stage visitor journey from first skeptical click to becoming an evangelist — was never committed to this repository as a standalone, unabridged document at any point before the Brain migration began. It is referenced by `../CHANGELOG.md` item 4, `../VISION.md`, and `../README.md`, but no file anywhere in the repository (including `FOUNDATION_REVIEW.md` and `FOUNDATION_REVIEW_FINAL.md`, which only name it in their scope statements) contains its actual 9-stage content.

Per `../GOVERNANCE/brain-governance-rules.md` rule 4, this gap is recorded rather than filled with newly-authored substitute text — authoring a new "experience blueprint" now, without the original 9 stages in hand, would be regenerating an approved Foundation artifact, not migrating one.

## What exists instead

`product-vision.md` §3 (Visitor Journey) covers the same subject from a functional rather than emotional angle — it is a distinct, already-migrated artifact, not a substitute for this one. Do not conflate the two when reading `../MODULE_INDEX.md` or `../VISION.md`.

## What this migration cannot do

Reconstruct the 9 emotional stages without the original document's text. See `../PROJECT_BRAIN_STATUS.md` for how this gap is tracked and what would close it.
