# Res Publica — Mission

*The organization's real, pre-existing mission — grounded in the actual project (README, `src/content/*/pages/about.mdx`, `mission.mdx`), not invented for this exercise. Every product decision in this Brain is downstream of this statement, not the reverse.*

Res Publica is a real German civic organization for **democracy, responsibility, dialogue, research, and public participation**. It is trilingual — German (primary), English, and Persian/Farsi (fully right-to-left, using the Solar Hijri calendar for dates) — reflecting an audience that includes German civic-engagement participants, international researchers and institutions, and Persian-speaking diaspora communities.

The organization runs on three pillars:

- **Research** — independent studies and policy analysis (e.g., on the digital public sphere, on participation impact).
- **Dialogue** — structured citizen participation formats ("Bürgerdialog") that produce concrete, publicly followed-up recommendations.
- **Participation** — projects that make civic processes and data genuinely usable by ordinary citizens (e.g., open municipal data made understandable without prior expertise).

The organization measures its own success in its own words: **"did a dialogue take place that would not have happened otherwise; did a decision become better informed"** — not attention, not engagement, not reach for its own sake. This single sentence is the reason `CONSTITUTION.md` §4 exists, and the reason Analytics (`BLUEPRINTS/mvp-module-blueprint.md` §9) explicitly excludes attention-style metrics rather than merely omitting them by accident.

It operates as a nonprofit, grant-funded organization, not a venture-backed startup — a fact that shapes the Business Model (`PRODUCTS/product-vision.md` §14), the resourcing assumptions throughout `KNOWLEDGE/operating-system.md`, and the phased, capacity-realistic roadmap in `ROADMAP.md`.

*See `VISION.md` for what this mission becomes as an AI-first civic platform, and `PRODUCTS/product-vision.md` for the full 15-section elaboration.*
