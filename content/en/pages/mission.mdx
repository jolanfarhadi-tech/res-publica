Our mission fits in one sentence: **We strengthen the democratic
public sphere.**

## Three goals

1. **Strengthen democracy** — through civic education, independent
   analysis, and the protection of open spaces for debate.
2. **Foster responsibility** — in institutions and individuals alike,
   from the municipality to the European level.
3. **Enable participation** — with formats that truly reach people:
   citizens' dialogues, workshops, open research.

## Our measure

We do not measure our work by attention, but by effect: did a
dialogue take place that would not have happened otherwise? Has a
decision become better informed, a process more open?
