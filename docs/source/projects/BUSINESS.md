**Version 1.0 proposal, pending approval:** a grant-funded nonprofit model (foundation and civic/government grants) as the primary revenue source, consistent with the organization's confirmed nonprofit status. A possible secondary channel: sliding-scale RPCS program fees, scaled to keep the program accessible rather than revenue-maximizing, consistent with the civic-access mission stated in `../foundation/00_MANIFESTO.md`. Membership/donation support follows the same model already defined for the Membership module in the organization's broader product ecosystem (`../foundation/06_ECOSYSTEM.md`). No revenue stream is ever derived from testimony or Responsibility Evidence content itself (`../governance/ETHICS_CHARTER.md`).
