# RPCS Levels

## Purpose
Describes the 5-level progression structure within RPCS.

## Background
Confirmed count (5 levels) from website-facing material. No level-by-level content document exists in the repository.

**Status: Version 1.0 placeholder.** Level count is confirmed; level names, entry/exit criteria, and content are not available in any source and are not invented here.

## Core Principles
Progression reflects genuine capability growth, never a gamified points/badge system (Core Principle 2 — Zero Gamification applies to RPCS exactly as it does everywhere else in the ecosystem).

## Definitions
A Level is a confirmed stage of capability within a Track — distinct from a Phase, which is a confirmed stage of the overall program timeline.

## Framework
5 levels.

**Version 1.0 proposal, pending approval:**
1. **Foundational Understanding** — grasp of HARM methodology and the participant's chosen Track.
2. **Guided Practice** — supervised practice of Track-specific skills.
3. **Independent Practice** — unsupervised practice, reviewed after the fact.
4. **Mentorship / Peer Review** — capability to support newer participants.
5. **Certified Practitioner** — full certification (see `RPCS_CERTIFICATION.md`).

## Workflow
Progression from Level 1 to Level 5 is sequential; completion is confirmed by a Facilitator at each level, never self-declared. *(Proposed structure, pending approval.)*

## Roles
**Participant** progresses through levels within their chosen Track.

## Inputs
Prior level completion (once defined).

## Outputs
Level completion, contributing toward Certification.

## Governance
No numeric score or rank is associated with level progression — completion is binary (met/not yet met), consistent with Zero Gamification.
