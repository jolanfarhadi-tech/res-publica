Describes the actual learning content delivered within RPCS.

## Background
No curriculum content document or fragment exists anywhere in the repository — only the program's structural counts (4 phases, 4 tracks, 5 levels) and track names are confirmed.

**Status: Version 1.0 proposal — structural skeleton only.** No specific curriculum content (readings, exercises, session plans) is invented here.

## Core Principles
Curriculum content must be trauma-informed, evidence-based, and directly tied to real HARM operational competence — consistent with the rest of the ecosystem's standing principles.

## Definitions
Curriculum content should logically correspond to each of the 4 confirmed tracks: Harm Explainer, AHIP Specialist, Codex Research, Trauma-Informed Facilitation.

## Framework
4 track-specific curricula, each spanning the program's phases/levels.

**Version 1.0 proposal, pending approval:**
- **Harm Explainer** — HARM methodology fundamentals; lay-accessible public communication of the Lifecycle and Innovations.
- **AHIP Specialist** — trauma-informed intake practice; safety triage; escalation protocols.
- **Codex Research** — taxonomy methodology; recurring-mechanism pattern analysis; evidence aggregation standards.
- **Trauma-Informed Facilitation** — Structured Hearing facilitation practice; de-escalation; Reflection-stage support.

## Workflow
Each track's curriculum is delivered across the program's confirmed 5 levels, progressing from foundational understanding to independent, certified practice (see `RPCS_LEVELS.md`). *(Proposed structure, pending approval.)*
