## Definitions
- **HARM** — the official name of Res Publica's operating methodology (not an acronym).
- **HARM analytical engine** — Harm, Accountability, Repair, Mobilization.
- **AHIP** — the trauma-informed intake process following Citizen Experience.
- **Structured Hearing** — the facilitated session following AHIP.
- **Harm Codex** — the standing taxonomy of recurring harm mechanisms.
- **Responsibility Evidence** — a verified record of a specific civic contribution.
- **Trust** — a qualitative civic property, never a numerical score. Never "Reputation."
- **Contribution** — a verifiable act of civic participation.
- **Impact** — the downstream, aggregate effect of verified Contribution.
- **RPCS** — Res Publica's Civic School (4 phases, 4 tracks, 5 levels).
- **Human Approval Authority** — the standing human role with sole approval authority; never an AI role.
