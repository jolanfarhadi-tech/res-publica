# AI Policy

## Purpose
States the operational policy governing AI use, as a governance document distinct from `../foundation/05_AI.md`'s foundational statement.

## Background
Reused directly from `../foundation/05_AI.md` and the organization's existing AI Governance Hierarchy — this document does not redefine either, only restates them in governance-process terms.

## Core Principles
See `../foundation/05_AI.md` — not restated in full here.

## Definitions
See `../foundation/05_AI.md`.

## Framework
Same Executive → Operational → Skills → Plugins → MCP Tools → Execution layering as `../foundation/05_AI.md`.

## Workflow
Any new AI capability proposal is reviewed against this policy before activation, following the organization's Agent Activation Roadmap discipline (map to an approved Operational Agent, or flag as requiring new approval).

## Roles
Human Approval Authority approves all AI capability activations.

## Inputs
A proposed AI capability or activation request.

## Outputs
An approval, rejection, or "not yet approved — requires new ADR" determination.

## Governance
This is a governance-process document; it does not itself grant any AI capability new authority.

## AI Integration
Self-referential — this document is itself part of what an AI capability proposal is checked against.
