# Manifesto

## Purpose
States Res Publica's founding purpose and north star in condensed form — the "why" every other document assumes.

## Background
Derived from Res Publica's existing, real mission and vision (not invented for this documentation effort).

## Core Principles
Democracy, responsibility, dialogue, research, and public participation.

## Definitions
Res Publica is a German civic organization, trilingual (German primary, English, Persian/Farsi RTL), nonprofit and grant-funded, measuring success by civic effect — not attention.

## Framework
Three pillars: **Research** (independent studies and policy analysis), **Dialogue** (structured citizen participation, "Bürgerdialog"), **Participation** (making civic processes and data genuinely usable by ordinary citizens).
