# Responsibility Biography Lab

## Purpose
Captures the raw, first-person account of lived civic experience — the first Innovation in the HARM framework, and the entry point to the entire HARM Lifecycle. Canonical name: **Responsibility Biography Lab** (UI label: "Biography Lab").

## Background
Specified in `docs/source/foundation/01_HARM_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md` (Innovation 1, described there as the "Citizen Experience" stage of the HARM Lifecycle). This document is the dedicated methodology-tier document for that Innovation, matching the treatment already given to Innovations 2–5 (`RESPONSIBILITY_MAPPING.md`, `RESPONSIBILITY_DASHBOARD.md`, `RESPONSIBILITY_ANNEXES.md`, `CIVIC_INTELLIGENCE.md`) — resolving Gap 1 identified in `docs/source/projects/INNOVATION_STRUCTURE.md` §5.

**Note on scope:** canonical source material describes this stage more minimally than Innovations 2–5 — it is the raw intake of an account, immediately followed by AHIP (`AHIP.md`). This document does not invent additional process detail beyond what `01_HARM_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md` and `HARM_LIFECYCLE.md` already establish.

## Core Principles
Human dignity — every account is treated as a real person's lived experience, never raw data (`01_HARM_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md` §Core Principles); nothing is filtered, categorized, or judged at this stage.

## Definitions
A Citizen Experience account is the raw, first-person account of something lived, shared before any structuring, verification, or categorization occurs. "Listening" is used elsewhere in canonical material (e.g., `RESPONSIBILITY_MAPPING.md` §Framework: "Input: one or more accounts from Listening/Citizen Experience") as a synonym for this same stage — not a distinct concept.

## Framework
- **Input:** a citizen's lived experience, shared through any Res Publica touchpoint.
- **Core Question:** "What happened, in this person's own words?"
- **Output:** a Citizen Experience account, ready for AHIP intake.

## Workflow
1. A citizen shares an account through any Res Publica touchpoint.
2. The account is received without filtering, categorization, or judgment.
3. The account moves to AHIP for trauma-informed intake (`AHIP.md`).

No further internal workflow is specified in canonical source material — this stage is intentionally minimal, since its entire function is to receive an account before any structuring begins.

## Roles
**Participant** — shares an account (`01_HARM_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md` §Roles). No other role acts at this specific stage; Moderator involvement begins at AHIP, not before.

## Inputs
A citizen's lived experience.

## Outputs
A Citizen Experience account, feeding AHIP.

## Governance
Subject to the same constitutional principles as the rest of HARM — zero gamification, no scoring or ranking of accounts, human dignity preserved throughout (`01_HARM_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md` §Governance).

## AI Integration
None specified at this stage in canonical source material. AI involvement, where it exists elsewhere in HARM, begins no earlier than AHIP (`AHIP.md`) — this document does not extend AI involvement earlier than what is already established.
