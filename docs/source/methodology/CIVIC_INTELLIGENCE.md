# Civic Intelligence

## Purpose
Synthesizes accounts, Responsibility Maps, Dashboard patterns, and Annexes into actionable collective understanding — the fifth and terminal Innovation. Canonical name: **Civic Intelligence Lab**.

## Background
Specified in `brain/FOUNDATION/01_HARM_OPERATING_SYSTEM.md` (Innovation 5).

## Core Principles
Synthesis grounded in accumulated evidence, never asserted independently; AI grounds and cites, never originates conclusions.

## Definitions
Civic Intelligence Reports and Recommendations are the outputs of this stage; both are Publications, grounded via Knowledge Assets and the AI Layer.

## Framework
- **Input:** the accumulated Story/account collection, Responsibility Maps, Dashboard patterns, and Annexes.
- **Core Question:** "What does the civic community now understand, and what should be done?"
- **Output:** Civic Intelligence Reports and Recommendations, entering the Knowledge Repository.

## Workflow
1. Synthesize across accumulated Knowledge Products.
2. Draft a Civic Intelligence Report and any Recommendations.
3. Human review and sign-off.
4. Publish to the Knowledge Repository, closing the loop back into future Citizen Experience intake.

## Roles
**Researcher, Expert** — synthesize. **Reviewer** — verifies. **Institution** — receives the output for Institutional Learning.

## Inputs
The accumulated Knowledge Product set.

## Outputs
Civic Intelligence Reports; Recommendations.

## Governance
No AI-originated conclusion is published without human sign-off (Core Principle 1).

## AI Integration
The AI Layer grounds retrieval and citation over the evidence base; it never originates a conclusion.
